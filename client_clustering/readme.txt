# Clustering: Marketing Campaign

This project applies unsupervised machine learning techniques to identify customer groups based on demographic information, purchasing behavior, and marketing campaign responses.

The objective of the project is to compare different clustering algorithms and evaluate their effectiveness in segmenting customers into meaningful groups that can support marketing analysis and business decision-making.

## Clustering Models Used
- K-Means
- Mean Shift
- DBSCAN
